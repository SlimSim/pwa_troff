Update Troff

Update on iOS

If you have problems downloading songs on iOS you may need to update your Troff version by clearing the website data.

Note: This will remove all data from Troff, so make sure your markers and songs are backed up somewhere so that you can extract it!
How to export and save your markers.

How to clear webb data on iOS

  1. Open the “Settings”
  2. Tap on “Safari”
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap on “Advanced”
  4. Tap “Website Data”
  5. Find troff.app in the list (if it is not in the list, you might have to press "Show All Sites" at the bottom)
  6. Tap “Edit” in the upper right corner,
  7. Tap the red (-) minus symbol next to troff.app followed by the “Delete” button".
  8. Go to Safari and reload you link to troff.app

Happy training!

Update on desktop and Android

It is generally easier to update Troff on Desktop and Android; when there is an update, Troff will load the updated version the next time you load the app. If this does not happen, you can try to reload the page or app, restart the browser or restart your device.

Note: This will not remove any data from Troff, however, the data saved in Troff is managed by the browser. If the browser needs more data and thinks that you Troff data is not regularly used, it may remove it.
